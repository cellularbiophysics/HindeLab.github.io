Join us!

We welcome motivated and creative individuals at all career stages, including Postdoctoral Fellows, Graduate Students, Visiting Graduate Students, and Undergraduate Students, to join our team.

Postdoctoral fellows

Interested candidates should directly email Elizabeth Hinde with your latest CV and a cover letter that briefly describes your previous accomplishments and why you are interested in our lab.

Graduate students

We are excited to welcome rotations from Unimelb graduate and MSc/PhD students in any department. Please directly email Elizabeth Hinde your CV and research interests.

Visiting graduate students

We also welcome visiting graduate students for research experiences at Unimelb. Please email Elizabeth Hinde your CV and broad research interests.

Undergraduate students

Students who are interested in joining the lab should directly contact Elizabeth Hinde with your CV.
